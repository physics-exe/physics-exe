Forecasting Reefer Power Peaks with a Lightweight Neural Generator

This site presents the physics.exe solution for the reefer peak-load challenge: a compact autoregressive notebook model, January forecast results, and the operational and weather signals that shaped the final predictions.

Explore January Rollout See Model Anatomy

Challenge Metric

0.5 × MAE all + 0.3 × MAE peak + 0.2 × pinball p90

What Had To Be Solved

The terminal needs a reliable view of tomorrow’s reefer demand

The challenge is about forecasting the combined electricity demand of plugged-in refrigerated containers. The goal is not just a good average prediction, but strong behavior during high-load hours and a useful upper-bound estimate for risk-aware planning.

In practice, that means combining operational state, seasonality, and weather context into a forecast that still stays simple enough to explain and present.
